Before Commuting From QC to Manila

When i was a child, i thought that commuting was not a big deal, i think of it as just a roadtrip since i am currently living in Novaliches, Quezon City where i dont get affected by the traffic because my school is a walking distance, from elementary to senior highschool. I wasn't aware about the traffic problems in the Philippines because i rarely go into Manila and go roadtrips.

Then one day, when i was about to graduate from senior highschool, i decided that i will be studying in University of the East Manila Campus, thats because it is one of the great university that specialize in IT. Since i wasn't aware, my parents and my older sister told me that Hindi biro ang bibiyahe ng malayo at araw-araw. Mahihirapan ka talaga diyan. Time is Gold at kailangan gumising ng maaga para hindi malate But i thought to my self thinking it can't be that bad, all i have to do is to just think of it like a roadtrip. But when i became a college freshmen, that is when i realized, everyday commuting is not a fun roadtrip and its tiring.

Thoughts About The Traffic Experience in Daily Commuting

Traffic is a common problem in the Philippines and is happening for a long time, where:

As someone who commutes everyday from Novaliches all the way to Morayta. My personal experience was very bad. Because why do we always have to face heavy rush hour traffic almost everyday just to go to school or work sweaty and smells a hint of polluted air. The cause of this is:

  • Short paths becomes long paths. There are also bottlenecks on the parts of the road as these bottlenecks makes traffic worse.
  • Rush hours usually happen during early in the morning, night, weekdays like monday, friday, and holidays such as christmas.
  • Traffic happens on Commonwealth, some parts of Quezon Avenue, Espana, Quirino Highway, Edsa, Rizal, and many more.

It wastes people's time because of traffic which i said Time isnt Gold, because it doesnt treasure time, especially a commuter that has super early schedule like 7am.

My Preferred Solutions in Traffic

I personally think the prior solution is that the government should prioritize on:

  • Reducing the traffic, and railway systems.
  • Improve the stoplight system, this is to create great flows and reduces traffic.
  • Improve the design model for transport vehicles like jeepney, and bus. Completely remove private bus companies, and instead turn it into government bus with a systematic routes that is literally free for our own benefits as a taxpayers, as an example from London bus.
  • Professionals and students schedules should not be very early and very late like we are some sort of robot, unlike some other countries that has great morning schedules.

For me, hybrid learning should be assessed properly, For example:

  • Schools should have 2 to 3 days online on weekday.
  • Colleges that doesnt have hands on activity should be like 2 weekdays online per week.
  • And work environment like tech companies should have 1 day onsite per week or even full work from home.

Time should be efficient and valuable like Gold. Time is meant to be wasted as it keeps moving forward and there is no turning back, if we let heavy traffic happen to us over and over again, alot of time will be waste and we tire ourselves from it.
